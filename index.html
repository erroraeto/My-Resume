Frontend Engineer

I am a passionate frontend developer as well as a 2D and 3D graphics visualizer.
My experience lies in creating website interfaces, realizing volumetric effects and website “life” effect.
I was also engaged in modeling of low- and high- poly models, their concepts & design, sculpting, retopology, unwrap, baking maps, texturing, rigging & skinning.
In the last three months I focused on honing my skills in creating a native and pleasantly perceived interface.
I am actively and rapidly exploring development frontend options to expand my ability to create extraordinary interfaces.
